Concept 1

A line is a collection of infinite points that travel forever in only two directions.

  • Example 1.1
  • Video
  • Interactive
  • Self-Check

Notice:

  1. The line in the graph is perfectly straight.
  2. The arrows at the ends of the line mean that the line continues forever in both directions.
  3. There are an infinite amount of points that lie on the line.
