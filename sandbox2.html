What Are Parallel Lines?

Instruction

Parallel lines are lines that:

  1. Do not intersect,
  2. Lie on one plane.

Parallel lines must travel in the same direction.

Parallel Graphed Lines

Instruction

Since parallel lines travel in the same direction, parallel lines must have the same or equal slopes.

Example

Steps to prove lines parallel on the coordinate plane:


  1. Show the slopes are equal
  2. Show that the y-intercepts are different. (If they are the same, then the lines lie on top of each other and create only one line.)
